# Comparable IPO Analyzer

## Peer selection

Use disclosed business model, revenue drivers, geography, stage, profitability, market cap, offer size, listing regime, and market period. Do not select peers from name similarity or sector buzzwords alone.

## Workflow

1. Define the comparison question and cutoff date.
2. Build candidate peers and document inclusion/exclusion reasons.
3. Require at least three strong peers when possible; otherwise label the set weak.
4. Normalize valuation dates, currencies, accounting periods, and return windows.
5. Compare offer valuation, subscription, cornerstone ratio, grey return, first-day return, and later performance where verified.
6. Use medians and ranges; show outliers and survivorship limitations.

## Required output

Provide a peer table with selection rationale and sources, median/range metrics, where the target differs, confidence, and the specific scoring or strategy inputs supported by the comparison.
