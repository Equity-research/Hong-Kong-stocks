# Listing Day Strategy

## Preconditions

Use only verified IPO identity, listing date, offer price, allocated lots, and current evidence. If allocation is unknown, write a conditional plan rather than assuming a position.

## Workflow

1. Summarize valuation, heat, allocation, grey-market, peer, and market-regime signals with timestamps.
2. Define bear/base/bull opening scenarios and observable triggers.
3. Specify actions by position type: cash allocation, financed allocation, or no allocation.
4. Include fee-adjusted break-even, financing cost, liquidity risk, and maximum acceptable loss.
5. Separate pre-open plan, opening-auction response, intraday reassessment, and invalidation conditions.
6. Avoid certainty, market orders in illiquid names, and strategies unsupported by the user's risk limits.

## Required output

Provide a compact decision table with scenario, trigger, action, size/lot scope, price or return zone, invalidation, and rationale. State that the plan is research support, not personalized investment advice.
